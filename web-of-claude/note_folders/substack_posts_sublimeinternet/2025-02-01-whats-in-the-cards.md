#### What’s in the Cards?

1. Chris Sacca (seed investor in Twitter, Uber, Instagram) on social media via the [Tim Ferriss podcast](https://tim.blog/2025/01/23/chris-sacca-2/):

   > I am a wealthy, happily married, got everything I need. Almost 50 year old, white dude. And when I get on Instagram, I feel so much fucking FOMO. My life feels so inadequate. I'm like, Jesus, look at that guy. Oh, fuck, where are they? They're having so much fun. Shit, that guy's so much fitter than me right now. Fuck. And it makes me unhappy. And so maybe me and 13 year old girls have a lot in common… We reinvented cigarettes, fentanyl lace cigarettes when we started social media with all the best intentions.

   P.S. I did not stop my car to jot this down. I captured it with Podcast Magic - the new podcast insight capture tool the Sublime team is developing. Email me if you want early access.

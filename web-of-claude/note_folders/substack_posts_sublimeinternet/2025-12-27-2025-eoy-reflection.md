# 2025 EOY Reflection

*State of Sublime: A Rare Peak Behind The Curtain*

Published: 2025-12-27
Source: https://sublimeinternet.substack.com/p/2025-eoy-reflection

I love when builders share the behind-the-scenes of what they’re making, while they’re making it.

So I tried to do the same for Sublime. I hit record and [talked through this past year](https://youtu.be/BnOGHVq8efA).

If you use [Sublime](https://sublime.app/), I think you’ll enjoy seeing the inner workings —where we are as a business, how we grow, how we’re feeling, etc… And if you’re an ambitious builder trying to create with a different relationship to time, you might feel mirrored here.

Mostly, I just want to say thank you for voting with your hearts and [wallets](https://sublime.app/pricing) for us to keep doing this work. Know that we don’t take your trust and attention lightly.

Beaming you all warm + sublime holiday wishes!

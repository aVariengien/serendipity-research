# Nothing against AI

*but the tools don't work the way the tweets say*

Published: 2025-07-19
Source: https://sublimeinternet.substack.com/p/nothing-against-ai

---

I have nothing against AI. Let me start there.

I wish I had a thousand hands and a thousand eyes.  
There is just… so much I want to do.

I want to grow [Sublime](https://sublime.app/) and make books and essays and chase so many rabbit holes. If AI can help me reach more people and do more of the things I love, I would be a huge beneficiary.

I’m not going to voluntarily handicap myself in the name of *purity*.

But I have been feeling incompetent lately and I finally put a finger on why.

I open X/Twitter and see

> "EVERYONE IS GROWING THEIR BUSINESS 10X FASTER WITH THIS AND THAT AI TOOL AND YOU SHOULD TOO."

But then I try the tools and it just doesn’t work the way the tweets say.

Here’s an example of what I mean:

I got a random email yesterday that mentioned Opencord —an auto-reply tool that’s supposed to promote your product by automatically replying to relevant tweets.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!06Kx!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff37a13e9-dcc8-4b2f-bcce-37e6f0a94fa6_1096x556.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!06Kx!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Ff37a13e9-dcc8-4b2f-bcce-37e6f0a94fa6_1096x556.png)

It looked sketchy but I had 20mins in between calls and I was feeling open to trying things. Also, [Podcast Magic](https://sublime.app/card/podcast-magic-is-the-simplest-way-to-capture-insig) is working brilliantly well and is only bottlenecked by people knowing it exists. If AI can help me get this tool in front of more people, I’d take the win.

I connect my Twitter account. It felt like giving my house keys to a stranger but hey, if they pay the rent, I’m not complaining.

##### WHO THE FUCK TALKS LIKE THIS?

Knowledge work is 90% reading the room—intuition, context, feel.   
I don’t want to spend my days hunting leads.   
But this isn’t it.

A few hours later, I’m back in a Slack thread debating the copy on a tooltip.  
Still sweating the color scheme of a landing page in Figma.  
Still staring at “Grow Podcast Magic” on my to-do list.  
(Speaking of, I’m looking for someone to work with me to grow Podcast Magic, creatively and operationally. If you’re a good fit, reply to this email.)

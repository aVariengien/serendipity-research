# The Sublime

Published: 2025-01-25
Source: https://sublimeinternet.substack.com/p/welcome-to-in-the-weeds

---

Welcome to the first episode of In the Weeds, a voice note experiment where I talk about the messy details, hard decisions, and real emotions of building sublime.app. While GOING THROUGH IT.

There will be no editing. Just me talking into a voice note and uploading it here. Next week, we'll figure out how to get this on Spotify and beyond.

I’ve always believed that if you have a container, you will make things to fill it. That the most crucial part of any project is to build a container for it. I’m so happy I now have a container to take you behind the scenes as we build this business. It’s [the bucket theory of creativity](https://sublimeinternet.substack.com/p/the-bucket-theory-of-creativity) in action.

Send me thoughts, feedback and questions anytime at sari@sublime.app.

See you next week!

# Defensive Knitwear

Published: 2023-01-24
Source: https://aifutures.substack.com/p/defensive-knitwear

---

Something different today: Clothing company [Cap\_able](https://www.capable.design/) has launched a line of knitwear that confuses AI\-driven facial recognition software. There have been other projects like this in the past but this one is actually kind of style.

“The camera will either fail to identify the wearer or it will think they are one of the animals embedded into the pattern which includes a zebra, a giraffe, or a dog, among other animals.” More on [Petapixel](https://petapixel.com/2023/01/20/this-clothing-line-tricks-ai-cameras-without-covering-your-face/?fbclid=IwAR2_41J8aye-YnGTV6mw1GZSKVJmMkjhan1IoOV5TgFXI6VKVEpacUemRhU)

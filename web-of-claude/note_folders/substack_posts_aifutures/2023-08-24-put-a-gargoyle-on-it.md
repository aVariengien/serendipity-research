Living in North America it’s hard not to wonder what happened to us as a civilization.

But I bring all this up because I’ve come across this project called [Monumental Labs](https://www.monumentallabs.co/), which is building “AI\-enabled robotic stone carving factories” so that we can “create cities with the splendor of Florence, Paris, or Beaux\-Arts New York, at a fraction of the cost.”

Monumental Labs is taking commissions and is looking to partner with builders and architects. If this project comes to fruition, in 20 years we could be living in a continent of midjourney experiments.

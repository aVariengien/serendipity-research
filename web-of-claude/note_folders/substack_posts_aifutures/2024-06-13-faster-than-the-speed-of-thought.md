# Faster than the speed of thought

Published: 2024-06-13
Source: https://aifutures.substack.com/p/faster-than-the-speed-of-thought

---

[Mat Dryhurst](https://www.instagram.com/matdryhurst/?hl=en) was on a panel I saw last week and brought up some questions that I think are fascinating:

How do we handle producing media faster than the speed of thought?

Models are taking 30 seconds to produce something that is 3 minutes in duration, and we’ve never dealt with that.

We are producing time.

If media is infinite, what is culture?

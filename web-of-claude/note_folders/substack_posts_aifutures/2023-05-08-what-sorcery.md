**Prediction**: writing an article or doing a presentation about ChatGPT and then dramatically revealing that you used ChatGPT to write it is going to age badly.

I think it will be like “I’m pretty sure you’re going to think I’m in a parallel universe here, but I used a word processor to write *this very article*.”

“I researched this topic\- *the topic we are literally talking about right now*  \- on the internet.”

“Are you sitting down? This morning I was able to make 15 exact copies of the document that you are holding in your hands.”

(No offence to those who have recently found themselves on the wrong side of history on this one.)

# Announcing a new print and digital collection

*For fans of galactic fragments*

Published: 2025-03-22
Source: https://aifutures.substack.com/p/announcing-a-new-print-collection

---

Diverging a little from my usual, I wanted to show some new things I’ve been working on. This series is called **Infinity Today.**

This collection is a series of text fragments set against the expanse of the galactic sky. While cosmic reflection has traditionally evoked the sublime and the unknowable vastness of the universe, in this series I wanted to use the sky as a stage for cultural references and technological concepts grounded in puns, syntactic glitches, and misfires.

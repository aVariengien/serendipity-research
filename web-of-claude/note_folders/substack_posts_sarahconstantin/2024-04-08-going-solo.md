So, as of now, I’ve left [Nanotronics.](https://nanotronics.co/)

It was a great experience — easily my favorite job environment, lifetime — and it was a privilege to work with such brilliant and dedicated people. I’m extremely grateful for my time there.

But it’s over, and now I’m getting a chance to focus more on my personal projects.

First of all, that means more writing, primarily on this Substack. Stay tuned for more frequent posts, and more subscriber\-only content.

Second of all, that means I’m now a lot more available for consulting.

I can do a variety of things:

* lit review and science writing for a general audience (similar to stuff you’ve seen on this blog)
* scientific due diligence on new technologies
* experimental design and generating strategic roadmaps
* evidence\-based analysis of medical/health interventions, especially for people weighing medical decisions or trying to make sense of a zoo of possible interventions for a chronic health issue
* data analysis
* market research and techno\-economic analysis
* and more! if you think there’s something I could do for you, let me know, even if it’s totally different from all of the above!

Here’s my [updated personal website](https://www.sarah-constantin.org/consulting), with more details about what I do.

You can [book me for a call](https://calendly.com/srconstantin) or just email me at srconstantin@gmail.com.

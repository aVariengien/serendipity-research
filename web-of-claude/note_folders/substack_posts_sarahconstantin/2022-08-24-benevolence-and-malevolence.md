One of the insights I got from [my version of “metta” meditation](https://sarahconstantin.substack.com/p/metta-meditation-my-way) was an introspectively clear sense that “benevolence” and “malevolence” are basically two disjoint worldviews. You’re either in a benevolent state, wishing well to yourself and more\-or\-less\-all others, or you’re in a malevolent state, wishing ill to yourself and more\-or\-less everyone.

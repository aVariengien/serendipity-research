**3:** You may have heard of [mirror life](https://www.smithsonianmag.com/smart-news/scientists-weigh-the-risks-of-mirror-life-synthetic-molecules-with-a-reverse-version-of-lifes-building-blocks-180987360/), a hypothetical form of synthetic microorganism based on reversed versions of biomolecules. If it were ever created - for example, by bioterrorists, risky research + lab leak, or a rogue AI - it could act as a sort of super-invasive species that would threaten most forms of normal life, including humans. The [Mirror Biology Dialogues Fund](https://www.mbdialogues.org/) is the main organization working to prevent its creation (you can learn more about them [on this 80K podcast](https://80000hours.org/podcast/episodes/james-smith-mirror-biology-catastrophe/)) and they ask me to announce that [they’re hiring](https://www.mbdialogues.org/jobs). Open roles include [deputy director](https://www.mbdialogues.org/jobs/deputy-director) and [people & programs operation lead](https://www.mbdialogues.org/jobs/people-program-ops-lead). Both roles are remote, salaries are $180K+ and $120K+ respectively, and first-round applications close 1/25 and 2/8, respectively.

**1:** [Inkhaven](https://www.inkhaven.blog/) was a blogging residency/bootcamp/program in Berkeley last November. The conceit was that residents had to write one post per day for thirty days, or else get kicked out without a refund. I ran some sessions, and so did other people you might recognize like Gwern, Zvi, Ozy, Aella, and Scott Aaronson. People seemed to like it (average rating 8/10, see also reflections [here](https://vishalblog.substack.com/p/for-your-consideration-inkhaven-2), [here](https://aelerinya.substack.com/p/inkhaven-was-all-i-wanted-and-more), [here](https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/hHf7jcW8keeCwFGAs/inkhaven-30-days-30-memories), [here](https://rivalvoices.substack.com/p/30-days-of-writing-my-inkhaven-experience), [here](https://signoregalilei.com/2025/12/07/looking-back-on-inkhaven/), [here](https://bengoldhaber.substack.com/p/thirty-reflections-from-thirty-days), [here](https://lettersfrombethlehem.substack.com/p/so-you-want-to-go-to-next-years-inkhaven), [here](https://www.mutuallyassuredseduction.com/p/tools-at-inkhaven-ranked-by-usefulness), [etc](https://www.inkhaven.blog/); when you make forty people write every day, you sure do end up with a lot of written reflections on the experience). They’re doing it again this April, and you’re invited to **[apply](https://www.inkhaven.blog/)**. You’ll need ~$3,500 (some scholarships available) and a month free. I plan to help again. Application deadline March 1.

**2:** ACX grantee [Aerolamp](https://aerolamp.net/) manufactures far-UVC lamps that kill airborne germs (but are safe for humans and animals). Place them in a heavily-trafficked area, and infections won’t spread from person to person because the germs will get zapped before they can reach a new host. The utopian dream is that nobody will have to worry about indoor gatherings during the next COVID-scale pandemic; more practically, companies and schools could use them to reduce sick days. In order to build buzz/awareness, Aerolamp will be giving away **free lamps** (~$500 value) to anyone with an appropriate location (they’re imagining coworking spaces and group houses, but maybe you have better ideas) who’s willing to display an included poster and take a customer survey. If you’re in this category, then **[apply here](https://aerodrop.org/)** for your free Aerolamp DevKit.

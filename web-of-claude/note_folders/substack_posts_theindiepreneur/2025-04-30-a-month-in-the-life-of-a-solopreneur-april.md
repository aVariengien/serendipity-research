**Today?**

* **75** users
* **5** actively paying users
* **5** testimonials and counting

Cost: Less than February.

I’ll give you a glimpse into how I did it (Will be detailed in full in the next post) and what I plan in the future.

#### How did I get to 5 paying customers

**DMs.**

Nothing special. Just sending honest DMs to people, starting a conversation and suggesting them an option to try WriteStack for FREE.

I started with giving 30 days for free with 250 credits.

Over 2 months it went down to 1 week and 10 credits, just to give them a taste of what WriteStack is and it’s value.

By giving people FREE trial, more than 50% followed up and even gave me feedback.

How many DMs? I sent **hundreds**. Every day for hours all I did was send DMs.

#### What are the plans for the future?

My current plans are to grow my affiliates ring, to get to as many people as possible and gather feedback.

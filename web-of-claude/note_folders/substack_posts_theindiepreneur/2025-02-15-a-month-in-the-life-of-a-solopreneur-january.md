## Tiny bets (Products I built)

### StripeWebhook (was: StripeProtect)

Another product, another mistake.

I made this mistake throughout all of my projects, and I finally realized my main problem.

I do not validate my ideas.

I just code and hope that I would find people who can use my products. Well, that has changed, and my new product has been validated.

That’s what happened with StripeProtect.

So now, I put it aside and put all my time and effort into marketing and iterating over my new product.

### Article Generator (No good name yet)

An ideas and outline generator for Substack readers.

I have a db of over 5 million Substack articles, and based on that and an analysis of your Substack, the tool produces ideas and outlines for new articles.

The point of it is not to make you redundant and write the article for you.

It’s meant for you to get started with a new idea and an outline, so you can fill in the blanks with your own words and writing.

I made a shift with this product.

After taking on marketing seriously and reading the same 5 books over and over, I decided to make a change in this product.

Instead of “shipping and see what sticks”, I moved to:

Validate→MVP→Cold/Warm DMs to people to give it a go → Iterate → Ship

We’ll so how it goes. So far I have 6 testers who try it and give me feedback.

I’ll keep you posted!

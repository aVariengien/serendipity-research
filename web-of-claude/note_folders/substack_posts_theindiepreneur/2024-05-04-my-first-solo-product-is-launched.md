Building a product alone is challenging.

* Do I need a logo?
* Is the URL catchy enough?
* Should the URL be catchy at this stage?
* You have no idea when enough is enough
* You don’t know what you should measure
* You always have second thoughts regarding the right time to publish
* You can’t not think that it might be a great success so you must go all in

And these are only things off the top of my head. I bet I can come up with a few dozen if I give it enough time.

The point is: **It’s scary. And exhausting.**

In the last 12 days, I spent between 11\-14 hours coding every day.

# PinkyPartner

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tWEI!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F45c7705b-988e-4d63-9f94-3358c1ba82b6_1916x959.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!tWEI!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F45c7705b-988e-4d63-9f94-3358c1ba82b6_1916x959.png)

[Put your pinky in!](https://www.pinkypartner.com)

Ever wanted to build a habit, but found it hard to do it alone? PinkyPartner solves your problem.

**PinkyPartner** allows you to create a contract between you and your friend that includes all the habits that you want to build and be accountable for each other.

Following each other’s progress adds extra social pressure to push each other to follow up on your promises and build your new habits!

This weekend I decided to try something different.

Something that was tried and tested before, by Lovable, and worked pretty damn good.

Instead of telling people how great WriteStack is, I will let them try it.

Completely free for an entire weekend.

—

In this email I’ll describe the experiment briefly and in the next one I’ll share the results, including raw numbers.

—

## What Is It.

It means exactly that.

Anybody can join

Anyone can sign up, jump in, and use all the best features of WriteStack without putting in a credit card for a free trial.

## Why?

3 reasons.

#### 1\. I want to check how will it convert.

I was told that it’s better to let users do a few things in the app before popping up the paywall.

It’s time to put it to the test.

#### 2\. Bring in more users

I trust WriteStack has reached a high level of self\-service.

Anybody who signs up can easily onboard themselves and try out all the features on their own.

And the value is **immense**.

And by showing people what WriteStack is all about, I know many will be convinced and continue for a free trial.

#### **3\. Virality chance**

A chance to go viral with this crazy offer and get more eyeballs than I’d imagined.

[👉 Sign Up To Free Weekend WriteStack](https://www.writestack.io/offers/interested/weekend)

## Goals

Simple: **get more people hooked.**

A free weekend lowers the barrier to entry, gets more creators to actually try the WriteStack, and nudges them toward becoming paid subscribers once they see the impact.

## Expectations

I don’t expect miracles, but I do expect movement.

If even a fraction of the free\-weekend users stick around and convert, that’s a win.

If the virality angle hits and people share it with their friends, that’s an even bigger win.

**Worst case**? I get a ton of feedback, new eyeballs, and more proof that WriteStack deserves attention.

## Final thought

This is the biggest event I have ever pulled.

I’m betting that once creators see what WriteStack can do, they won’t want to go back.

WriteStacks speaks louder than any sales pitch, and giving people an easy way to experience it is the fastest way to grow.

So if you’ve been sitting on the fence, this is your shot.

An entire weekend to use WriteStack. No credit card, no excuses. Try it. Break it. Push it to its limits.

I am here to listen to everything you have to say!

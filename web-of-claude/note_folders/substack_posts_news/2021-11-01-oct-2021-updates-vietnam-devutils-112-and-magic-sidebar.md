### 🐍 Fun stuff I did with my banner (again)

This idea has stuck in my head for a while, I decided to finally do it: build a Snake game on my Twitter banner!

[![I let people play snake on my Twitter banner](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Dlub!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_lossy/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7b7d559f-a2e2-4f8b-bb8d-1d5a276d58dc_514x294.gif "I let people play snake on my Twitter banner")](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Dlub!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F7b7d559f-a2e2-4f8b-bb8d-1d5a276d58dc_514x294.gif)

I let people play snake on my Twitter banner

The way it works:

* Someone comment ➡️⬇️⬅️⬆️ emojis to my tweets
* I use Twitter API to read the emojis
* Use the commands from the emojis to control the snake
* Then I update the latest game state to my Twitter banner.

It’s fun! 😂

The tweet got 675 replies, the most replies I ever had in 1 single tweet ever!

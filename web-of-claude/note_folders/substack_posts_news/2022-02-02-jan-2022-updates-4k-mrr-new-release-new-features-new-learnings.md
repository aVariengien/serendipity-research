## 📈 I learned to track conversion rate

Up until now, I have never set up anything to track the conversion rate for Black Magic. I was 100% focused on building.

Now that the product has picked up some momentum, I started to spend some time setting up proper tracking and picking some low-hanging fruits that will help with conversion.

Here are some interesting learnings I want to share with you:

**Only ~58% of people complete the sign-up flow. Only 23% actually started using the sidebar.**

[![Some funnel I set up. I use Mixpanel for this.](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!AeIe!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fda9cb5d8-4a54-4705-8c26-4e2ca97c14e6_1608x940.png "Some funnel I set up. I use Mixpanel for this.")](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!AeIe!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fda9cb5d8-4a54-4705-8c26-4e2ca97c14e6_1608x940.png)

Some funnel I set up. I use Mixpanel for this.

It’s quite bad. I don’t know why. It’s worst when I think about how many missed opportunities with such a high drop-off. But hey, at least I’m learning 😂

I think the reason may be people accidentally click the sign-in button, or they get scared away by the list of permissions requested from Twitter (which is a [painful problem I cannot fix](https://tdinh.notion.site/Why-BlackMagic-so-needs-so-many-permissions-d6aa0c3340b044e3bde5d63cd0084978?utm_campaign=Tony%20Dinh%E2%80%99s%20Newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter)).

I’ll look for ways to find out the reasons soon.

**Only 36% of people finish the onboarding flow after they started**

[![Onboarding flow funnel](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!t97v!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F660601e1-14a0-42c6-9f72-202dad30a6cf_1591x933.png "Onboarding flow funnel")](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!t97v!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F660601e1-14a0-42c6-9f72-202dad30a6cf_1591x933.png)

## 👣 My journey from 0 → 20K followers

My last newsletter issue got to the [front page of Hacker News](https://twitter.com/tdinh_me/status/1478598541593354240?utm_campaign=Tony%20Dinh%E2%80%99s%20Newsletter&utm_medium=email&utm_source=Revue%20newsletter). A lot of people asked me how I grow my audience from 0 → 20K followers in 2021.

In short, my story can be summarized into 4 bullet points:

* Engage with people
* Do cool/creative stuff, make noise
* Build products in public and fail in public
* Be genuine, be nice to people, and spread positivities

Somewhere along with those steps, I got lucky. A few times.

For example, this tweet got me 4,000 followers within 48 hours. I only had ~1,000 followers at the time.

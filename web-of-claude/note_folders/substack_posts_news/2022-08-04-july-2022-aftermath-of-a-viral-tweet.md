## 🔥 Aftermath of a viral tweet

In July, Black Magic was mentioned in [a viral tweet](https://twitter.com/matt_gray_/status/1548296795096096770) 🔥

Website traffic:

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Rnde!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb08c529a-1c65-4852-b1cb-f3dbfb9c1fb2_2352x994.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Rnde!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fb08c529a-1c65-4852-b1cb-f3dbfb9c1fb2_2352x994.png)

Brought in a lot of visitors, and ~**1,500 new users**.

At peak, there were 689 new users in a single day.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2F2g!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F01435136-2ce5-4cbd-b053-6b66f33b3fee_1340x936.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2F2g!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F01435136-2ce5-4cbd-b053-6b66f33b3fee_1340x936.png)

I was super excited! 😱

My usual Sign Up → Subscribe conversion rate is about 4%.

So let’s do some math 📐

If I convert **4% of these 1,500 new users**, that means I’ll have **~60 new subscribers.**

And if each user contributes **$14 MRR** on average…

That means I will have **+$840 new MRR**, right? 😱

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!L8kz!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5a15eed7-da86-49b3-aa13-4e80fc75e951_500x500.jpeg)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!L8kz!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F5a15eed7-da86-49b3-aa13-4e80fc75e951_500x500.jpeg)

Well, the answer is… no.

I got **9 new subscribers** (so far). The conversion rate is about **0.6%**.

I’m still very happy! But still somewhat disappointed.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2Gss!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0c468ad0-106d-4add-b33b-4ecb5816a652_2280x982.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!2Gss!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F0c468ad0-106d-4add-b33b-4ecb5816a652_2280x982.png)

Maybe they have not converted yet?

It’s been ~20 days since they signed up, 4 days passed their trial period.

If they want to subscribe, they would have already.

Besides, on average, **45%** of my customers subscribe in the first **2 days**.

More than **75%** of customers subscribe before **day 17**.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ewaN!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbac109f2-e10e-47c1-9d76-de7732b50ee6_1328x1090.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!ewaN!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fbac109f2-e10e-47c1-9d76-de7732b50ee6_1328x1090.png)

This is “time to convert” distribution

Now on day 20, I don’t think I will be able to convert those 1,500 new users anymore.

Maybe when I release a killer feature in the future, they’ll come back. But as of now, I think that’s all.

### 🤔 So why the conversion rate is suddenly so low?

I have no idea, but I have a guess.

The [viral tweet](https://twitter.com/matt_gray_/status/1548296795096096770) was tweeted by [Matt](https://twitter.com/matt_gray_) (thank you, Matt! ❤️).

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8xAX!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fff99f1a6-3f55-48dc-acaf-790b8bf14a57_1476x1274.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!8xAX!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fff99f1a6-3f55-48dc-acaf-790b8bf14a57_1476x1274.png)

By looking at the data, I can clearly see that we have a **very small audience overlap**.

Only **2,775 common followers**.

[![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!o-Ao!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F202d622e-e20c-40de-a1a6-33f8c0f4a189_862x926.png)](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!o-Ao!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F202d622e-e20c-40de-a1a6-33f8c0f4a189_862x926.png)

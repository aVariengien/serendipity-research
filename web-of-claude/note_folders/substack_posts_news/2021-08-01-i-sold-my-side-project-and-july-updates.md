## 💰 I sold DriveStats.io on Micro Acquire

This is a project I have been working on since April-May 2021. I recently posted the product to [MicroAcquire](https://microacquire.com/). Within 48 hrs, I confirmed a deal with a buyer. After 10 days, the deal is closed!

Some quick numbers about DriveStats.io stats:

* 239 scans
* 164 users
* $53 revenue

Cost:

* $40/year for domain
* $60/year for server

Deal amount: **$1,800**

This is not a lot of money (far from it), however, I learned a lot from the selling process on MicroAquire. I wrote a lengthy thread about it below. Check it out if you are interested! 👇

## 💡 This month’s inspiration for your product ideas

**You don’t have to be a Machine Learning Engineer to build an AI app.**

With [Open AI](https://openai.com/), you can leverage the power of GPT-3 and build apps around it without having to understand how neural networks or machine learning works. Yes, that’s right.

It will be soon a general technology that every developer can use, so now is the best time for you to catch the wave! I have requested and was granted to the private beta API, it [blew my mind](https://twitter.com/tdinh_me/status/1404305604135460864) on the AI’s capability. I encourage you to do the same too!

The Open AI team is likely to approve your application if you have a specific app you want to build in mind, so come prepared!

The best way to find an idea is to read through their [documentation](https://beta.openai.com/docs/introduction) and [examples](https://beta.openai.com/examples), it will give you the best understanding of what the AI can and cannot do.

Below are some of the successful indie products I know that are using Open AI, for your inspiration:

* [Copy.AI](https://www.copy.ai/): help users with marketing copy. By [Paul](https://twitter.com/PaulYacoubian) and [Chris](https://twitter.com/chris__lu). Making 💵 **$140k MRR(!!)**
* [ContentBot.ai](https://contentbot.ai/): help users with blog posts, landing pages, and ad copy. By [Nick](https://twitter.com/Nicholas_Duncan). Making 💵 **$2.3k MRR**.

Good luck with your idea!
